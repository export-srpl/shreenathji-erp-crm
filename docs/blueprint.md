# **App Name**: Shreenathji ERP

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Roles: Role-based access control with pre-defined roles: Admin, Purchase, Production, QC, Sales, Logistics, Finance, and Customer.
- Deals/Pipeline Management: Kanban-style pipeline view to track deal stages (Prospect, Quotation, Negotiation, Won/Lost).
- Purchase & Inventory: Manage vendors, purchase orders, goods receipts, and raw material stock levels. Support for import tracking and pricing history.
- Production & Manufacturing: Manage BOM, production planning, batch tracking, machine utilization. Integration with QC for approvals.
- Quality Control (QC & Compliance): Implement QC checklists, COA generation, document storage, and status tracking. Ensure export compliance.
- Finished Goods Inventory: Manage finished goods inventory with multi-warehouse support, FIFO/LIFO valuation, and expiry tracking.
- AI-powered Form Generation: Auto-generation of various forms (including COA, TDS, Proforma Invoice, Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Delivery Challan) by LLM tool to ingest various information about parameters from the product and then deciding whether to incorporate a standard disclaimer (e.g. for safety)

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A muted green (#8FBC8F) to evoke trust, growth, and stability, aligning with the company's focus on chemicals.
- Background color: A light, off-white (#F5F5F5) to ensure readability and a clean, professional look.
- Accent color: A desaturated blue (#A9BCD0) to complement the green and provide a calm, reliable feel, useful for calls to action and important information.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, which combines a modern look with a little warmth or personality; suitable for headlines or body text
- Clean and structured layout with clear visual hierarchy to ensure ease of use and quick access to key information. Use of cards to display key modules and reports.
- Use consistent and professional icons to represent different modules and actions. Icons should be easily recognizable and aligned with the app's overall style.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience without being distracting. For example, smooth transitions between pages and micro-interactions on button presses.
